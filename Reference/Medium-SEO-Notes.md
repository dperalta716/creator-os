# Medium SEO Notes

## Key Facts
- Medium has high domain authority — articles rank faster than a new blog
- No need for Medium Partner Program ($5/mo) — use Medium as traffic source, not revenue
- Can post up to 2 articles per 24-hour period
- 80% of Gumroad traffic (for Travis model) comes from Medium articles

## Optimization Tips
- SEO-friendly titles with keywords
- Keyword in first paragraph
- Use H2 subheadings with keyword variations
- 800-1500 words optimal for Medium
- Embed Gumroad links at beginning, middle, and end
- Include email signup CTA
- Add relevant Medium tags (up to 5)
